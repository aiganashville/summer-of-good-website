About Summer of Good

Summer of Good is AIGA Nashville's take on the national AIGA Design for Good effort.

Over the course of eight weeks this summer, four teams comprised of design students and professional mentors (professional designers working in Nashville) will be paired with select non-profit organizations to address the organizationâ€™s design needs by applying design thinking, problem solving, and technical design skills. Each team will be assisted by an AIGA facilitator.

At the end up the Summer of Good program, AIGA Nashville will host a reception open to all from the Nashville design, non-profit and business communities, where the design teams will present the work they have accomplished.
